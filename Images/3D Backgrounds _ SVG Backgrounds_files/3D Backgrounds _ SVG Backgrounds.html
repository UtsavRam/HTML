3D Backgrounds

Achieving three-dimensional design is tricky in vector software, since most tools manipulate 2D space. The designs in this series use a few tricks to achieve the illusion of depth: tints, shades, shadows, and perspective. 3D backgrounds can lift content off the page to bring attention to critical information.
